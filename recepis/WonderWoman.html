WonderWoman

Ingredients:

60 ml gin
1/2 medium-sized grapefruit
A few sprigs of fresh rosemary
Ice cubes
Tonic water
Grapefruit slices or sprigs of rosemary for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

FStart by squeezing the juice from half a grapefruit into a glass or cocktail shaker. If you prefer a sweeter taste, you can also muddle some of the grapefruit pulp. Take a few sprigs of fresh rosemary and gently crush them with your hands to release the oils and fragrance. Add them to the grapefruit juice. Pour 60 ml of gin into the glass or cocktail shaker with the grapefruit juice and rosemary. Fill the glass or shaker with ice cubes and stir or shake well to mix the ingredients and chill the drink. If desired, strain the mixture into a separate glass filled with fresh ice cubes. Alternatively, you can leave the drink in the original glass with the ice. Add some tonic water to give the drink effervescence and a slightly sweet note if desired. You can adjust the amount of tonic water according to your taste. Gently stir the cocktail to mix the tonic water with the other ingredients. Garnish the drink with a slice of grapefruit or a sprig of rosemary for an additional elegant touch. Enjoy your Grapefruit Rosemary Gin Cocktail!
