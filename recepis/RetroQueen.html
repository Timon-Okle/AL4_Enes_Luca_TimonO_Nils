RetroQueen

The Retro Queen is a refreshing drink that with its fruity flavors and sparkling character is the perfect choice for a relaxed evening. Here's the recipe:

Ingredients:

3 cl Aperol
6 cl Prosecco
1 splash of soda water
1 orange slice
Some ice cubes

Instructions:

Fill a glass with some ice cubes to keep the drink cool. Pour 3 cl Aperol over the ice cubes. Pour 6 cl Prosecco into the glass. Add a splash of soda water to give the drink a light carbonation. Decorate the drink with an orange slice for a fresh and appealing look.
