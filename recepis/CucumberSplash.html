CucumberSplash

Instructions:

To prepare the Cucumber Splash, mix 45 ml vodka, 30 ml lemon juice, 15 ml cucumber juice, and 15 ml simple syrup in a cocktail shaker. Shake the ingredients vigorously to mix and chill them. Remove the ice from a pre-chilled cocktail glass and pour the contents of the shaker into it. Top up the glass with soda water to add fizziness to the drink. Stir gently and garnish the cocktail with cucumber and lemon slices. Serve it chilled and enjoy the drink.
